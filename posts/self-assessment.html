Self Assessment

to attain?
2. Actively engage my students in the learning activities
that I include in my teaching plan/syllabus?
3. Create a positive classroom climate for learning?
4. Reflect on my teaching practice with the aim of making
appropriate improvements?
5. Develop different assessment methods depending on
the learning goals I want to check in my students?
